How It Works

1

Make An Order

We are always ears for your calls. As a customer you can contact us anytime to place an order. We will need the information like the pick-up location , delivery location, your name, phone number etc.

2

Make Partial Payment

After placing your order, you have to make a 50% payment of the transport fees in advance. This is just a royality fees collected and will be refunded if we fail to deliver the package. After payment our team will arrive at your location on the sheduled date and wrap all your items securely and pack each of them with writings to identify later.

3

Delivery

The packages will be loaded and shipped to the agreed order location. The delivery will be made on time as promised. The balance payment has to be made at this time. You can always call to enquire about the current location of your packages.
